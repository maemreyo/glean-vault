- Recovery: recover vs heal vs rest

- Studying: learn vs memorize vs understand
- Testing: pass vs succeed vs ace

- Cooking: prepare vs cook vs make
- Cleaning: tidy vs clean vs wash
- Shopping: buy vs purchase vs get
