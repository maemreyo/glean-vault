[[20_Vocabulary/reindeer|Reindeer]] (or caribou) have to [[30_Structures/live through prolonged seasons of darkness|live through prolonged seasons of darkness]] during Arctic winters (it stays darker longer), so God provided them with the ability to change their whole eye color. Their eyes turn blue, increasing their [[30_Structures/visual sensitivity|visual sensitivity]] by up to 1,000 times, allowing them to detect predators in low-light conditions.

In summer, their eyes shift back to gold to handle [[30_Structures/the glare of endless daylight|the glare of endless daylight]]. We can clearly see God's [[20_Vocabulary/provision|provision]] and planning!
