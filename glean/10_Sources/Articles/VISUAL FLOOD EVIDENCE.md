This is Ireland’s Dún Briste, [[30_Structures/a towering 150-foot sea stack|a towering 150-foot sea stack]] standing just off the coast. [[30_Structures/It is a striking reminder|It is a striking reminder]] that the Earth’s surface can change rapidly and [[20_Vocabulary/catastrophically|catastrophically]]. [[30_Structures/The sheer vertical cliffs|The sheer vertical cliffs]], sharp edges, and clearly visible rock layers all point to rapid formation, not slow and gentle processes stretched over millions of years.
