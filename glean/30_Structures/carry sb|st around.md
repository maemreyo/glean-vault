carry sb|st around
> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Phrasal Verb
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Subject] + carry + [somebody/something] + around
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **"carry"**: Main verb meaning to support and move
> - **"sb/st"**: Object (somebody/something)
> - **"around"**: Adverbial particle indicating movement in various places
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Object can be placed between "carry" and "around" (separable)
> 2. Can also be used as: carry around [object]
> 3. Requires a physical object or person as object

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** To transport something/someone with you to various places
> - **Figurative:** To keep something constantly (memories, emotions, ideas)
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To indicate continuous possession or transportation
> - **Nuance:** Can imply burden, convenience, or constant companionship
>
