a wolf spider
> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Noun Phrase with Indefinite Article
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [a] + [wolf] + [spider]
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **"a"**: Indefinite article indicating one instance
> - **"wolf"**: Noun used as adjective modifier (descriptive)
> - **"spider"**: Head noun (main subject)
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Article "a" not "an" (begins with consonant sound)
> 2. "Wolf" functions as adjective but remains noun form
> 3. No hyphen required between "wolf" and "spider"

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** One spider of the species Lycosidae
> - **Figurative:** Something that hunts actively like a wolf
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To identify a specific spider species
> - **Nuance:** Scientific/common name combination
