> ```text

> **Components:**

> - **Component 2:** Adjective "relative" - modifies the type of humidity
> - **Component 3:** Noun "humidity" - the main concept
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Use "the" when referring to specific or previously mentioned humidity
> 2. "Relative" modifies humidity to indicate comparison to maximum possible
> 3. Pattern extends to: the + [descriptive adjective] + [scientific/technical noun]

> - **Literal:** The amount of water vapor present in air expressed as a percentage of the maximum amount the air could hold at that temperature
> - **Figurative:** Can metaphorically describe emotional or atmospheric tension levels

> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To specify a measurable environmental condition
> - **Nuance:** Technical/scientific register, precise measurement
