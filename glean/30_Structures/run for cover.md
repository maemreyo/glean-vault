> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Idiomatic Expression (Verb + Preposition + Noun)
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Subject] + run + for + cover
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Verb (run):** Action of moving quickly on foot
> - **Preposition (for):** Indicates purpose or destination
> - **Noun (cover):** Protection, shelter, or hiding place
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Intransitive expression - doesn't take a direct object
> 2. Usually used as an imperative or with subject pronouns
> 3. Can be modified with adverbs: "run quickly for cover"

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** To run to find shelter from physical danger (rain, attack, etc.)
> - **Figurative:** To escape from a difficult situation or avoid confrontation
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To express urgent need for protection or escape
> - **Nuance:** Implies sudden danger and immediate reaction
>
