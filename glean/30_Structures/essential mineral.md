# essential mineral

> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Adjective + Noun Pattern
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Adjective] + [Noun]
> essential + mineral
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** [Adjective] (essential) - indicating necessity/importance
> - **Component 2:** [Noun] (mineral) - the substance type being described

> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Simple adjective-noun modification
> 2. "Essential" emphasizes that the body cannot produce this mineral
> 3. Often appears in plural: "essential minerals"
> 4. Technical/scientific collocation with specific meaning in nutrition

> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** A mineral required by the body for proper functioning that must be obtained from diet
> - **Figurative:** Something fundamentally necessary for success or survival
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To identify nutrients that cannot be synthesized by the body
> - **Nuance:** Scientific/medical context, implies health importance
