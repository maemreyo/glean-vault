> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Compound Noun Phrase / Scientific Term
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [material name] + [form/type]
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** Material modifier "selenite" - specifies the type of crystal
> - **Component 2:** Head noun "crystal" - identifies the object category
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Material/type name comes before the category noun
> 2. No article needed when using as general term
> 3. Can take article "a/the" when referring to specific instances
> 4. Pattern extends to: [mineral/gem name] + crystal/stone/mineral
