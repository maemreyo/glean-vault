eggsac
> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Compound Noun
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [egg] + [sac]
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **"egg"**: Noun indicating reproductive cell
> - **"sac"**: Noun meaning pouch-like container
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Always written as one word or hyphenated (egg-sac)
> 2. Functions as a single noun unit
> 3. Plural form: eggsacs (or egg-sacs)

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** A protective case containing eggs laid by spiders, insects, or other animals
> - **Figurative:** Container of potential or beginnings
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** Scientific/biological term for egg container
> - **Nuance:** Technical term used in zoology
