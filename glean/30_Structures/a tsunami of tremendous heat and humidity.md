> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Extended Metaphor / Descriptive Phrase
>

> ```text
> [Indefinite Article: a] + [Metaphor: tsunami] + [Preposition: of] + [Intensifier: tremendous] + [Nouns: heat and humidity]
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** `a tsunami` - natural disaster metaphor suggesting overwhelming force
> - **Component 2:** `of tremendous heat and humidity` - the actual conditions being described
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Natural disaster metaphor + "of" + intense weather conditions
> 2. Requires "tremendous" or similar intensifier to justify "tsunami" comparison
> 3. Heat and humidity are parallel elements joined by "and"

> - **Figurative:** An intense, pervasive atmosphere of discomfort
