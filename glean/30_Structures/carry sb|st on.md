carry sb|st on
> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Phrasal Verb (Multiple Meanings)
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Subject] + carry + [somebody/something] + on
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **"carry"**: Main verb meaning to support and move
> - **"sb/st"**: Object (somebody/something)
> - **"on"**: Adverbial particle with multiple meanings
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Separable: object can go between carry and on
> 2. Intransitive use: carry on (without object) means continue
> 3. Context determines meaning (physical vs figurative)

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Physical:** To support and transport someone/something
> - **Figurative:** To continue doing something; to have an affair
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** Physical transport, continuation, or secret relationship
> - **Nuance:** Highly dependent on context and sentence structure
