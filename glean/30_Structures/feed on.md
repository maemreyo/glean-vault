# feed on

> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Phrasal Verb Pattern
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Subject] + feed + on + [Object/Noun Phrase]
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** [Verb] (feed) - the action of eating
> - **Component 2:** [Particle] (on) - indicating the food source
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. "Feed" followed by preposition "on"
> 2. "On" introduces the food source or what is consumed
> 3. Can be used for animals or metaphorically for humans/ideas
> 4. Transitive phrasal verb - requires object

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** To eat a particular type of food as one's main diet
> - **Figurative:** To use something as a source of energy, motivation, or inspiration
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To describe dietary habits or sources of sustenance/inspiration
> - **Nuance:** Can imply dependence or regular consumption
>
