scatter in all directions
> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Phrasal Verb / Idiomatic Expression
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Subject] + scatter + in + all + directions
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **"scatter"**: Main verb meaning to separate and go in different ways
> - **"in all directions"**: Prepositional phrase indicating complete dispersal
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Subject is usually plural or a collective noun
> 2. "in all directions" is a fixed phrase
> 3. Can be used in active or passive voice

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** To move quickly apart in every possible direction
> - **Figurative:** To spread widely, disperse information/attention
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To describe rapid, chaotic movement or spreading
> - **Nuance:** Often implies urgency, panic, or sudden action
>
