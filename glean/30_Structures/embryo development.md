embryo development
> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Compound Noun (Scientific Terminology)
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Noun] + [Noun]
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** Noun "embryo" - specifies the biological stage/organism
> - **Component 2:** Noun "development" - indicates the process of growth/change
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. First noun acts as adjective (attributive noun) specifying type
> 2. No preposition or possessive needed between compound nouns
> 3. Second noun is the head noun (main concept)
> 4. Stress typically falls on first noun in speech: EM-bryo development

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** The process by which an embryo grows and develops
> - **Figurative:** The early stages of any developmental process
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To describe the biological process of embryonic growth
> - **Nuance:** Scientific register, precise biological terminology
