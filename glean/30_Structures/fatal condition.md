fatal condition
> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Compound Noun Phrase / Medical Term
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Adjective: fatal] + [Noun: condition]
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** `fatal` - adjective causing or resulting in death
> - **Component 2:** `condition` - noun referring to a state of health or situation
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Standard adjective-noun order
> 2. Often used in medical or emergency contexts
> 3. Can be modified by intensifiers (e.g., "ultimately fatal")

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** A medical state or situation that will lead to death
> - **Figurative:** A circumstance that ensures failure or destruction
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To indicate extreme danger or inevitability of negative outcome
> - **Nuance:** Serious, formal, medical or dramatic
>
