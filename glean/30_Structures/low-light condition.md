> ```text

> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** Hyphenated Adjective "low-light" - describing minimal illumination
> - **Component 2:** Noun "condition" - referring to a state or circumstance
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. "Low-light" hyphenated when preceding noun as compound adjective
> 2. No hyphen when used separately: "The light is low"
> 3. Compound adjective permanently modifies the noun

> - **Figurative:** Unclear or difficult to see circumstances
