the creepy crawlies
> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Idiom / Fixed Expression
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [the] + [creepy] + [crawlies]
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **"the"**: Definite article indicating a specific category
> - **"creepy"**: Adjective meaning causing fear or disgust
> - **"crawlies"**: Plural noun referring to small crawling creatures
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Always used with "the" - it's a fixed expression
> 2. "Crawlies" is always plural and informal
> 3. Functions as a collective noun for insects and arachnids

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** Small crawling creatures that cause unease
> - **Figurative:** Things that make you uncomfortable or scared
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To express fear or disgust about insects/spiders
> - **Nuance:** Informal, often used with humor or exaggeration
