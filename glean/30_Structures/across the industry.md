across the industry
> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Prepositional Phrase
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> across + the + industry
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **"across"**: Preposition indicating spanning or throughout
> - **"the"**: Definite article specifying particular industry
> - **"industry"**: Noun referring to business sector
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Functions as adverbial phrase modifying verb/adjective
> 2. Always uses definite article "the"
> 3. Can be replaced with specific industry name (the tech industry)

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** From one side to the other of an industry
> - **Figurative:** Throughout an entire sector or field
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To indicate comprehensive scope within business sector
> - **Nuance:** Professional, business contexts, emphasizes breadth
>
