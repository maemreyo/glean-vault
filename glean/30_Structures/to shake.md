to shake
> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Verb / Phrasal Verb Pattern
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Infinitive Marker: to] + [Verb: shake] (+ [Optional: Object/Adverb])
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** `to` - infinitive marker indicating purpose or direction
> - **Component 2:** `shake` - verb meaning to move back and forth quickly
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Base form "shake" (not "shakes" or "shook") after "to"
> 2. Can be transitive (with object) or intransitive (without object)
> 3. Often appears in idiomatic expressions

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** To move something back and forth or up and down with quick movements
> - **Figurative:** To upset, shock, or undermine someone's confidence or beliefs
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To describe physical movement or emotional impact
> - **Nuance:** Can be gentle or violent, literal or metaphorical
