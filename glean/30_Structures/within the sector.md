> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Prepositional Phrase (Preposition + Article + Noun)
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> within + the + sector
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Preposition (within):** Indicates inside boundaries or limits
> - **Article (the):** Definite article specifying particular sector
> - **Noun (sector):** Distinct part or division of economy/industry
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. "Within" always requires "the" when referring to specific sectors
> 2. Functions as adverbial phrase showing location/scope
> 3. Emphasizes being inside boundaries, not necessarily everywhere

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** Inside the boundaries of a specific sector
> - **Figurative:** Belonging to or contained within a particular field
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To specify location or scope within defined boundaries
> - **Nuance:** Indicates containment but not necessarily universal coverage
>
