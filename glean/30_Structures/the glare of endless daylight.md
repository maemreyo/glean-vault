> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Article] + [Noun] + [Preposition] + [Adjective] + [Noun]

> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** Definite Article "the" - specific instance
> - **Component 2:** Noun "glare" - intense, blinding light
> - **Component 3:** Preposition "of" - showing relationship/origin
> - **Component 4:** Adjective "endless" - seeming to have no end

> 1. Definite article indicates specific glare being discussed
> 2. Preposition "of" connects source to phenomenon
> 3. Adjective precedes noun it modifies
> 4. Creates evocative imagery through word combination
