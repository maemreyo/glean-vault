> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Phrasal Verb
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Subject] + measure up + [optional: to + standard/expectation]
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** Verb "measure" - to assess, evaluate, or determine
> - **Component 2:** Particle "up" - indicates completion or reaching a standard
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Can be transitive (measure someone/something up) or intransitive
> 2. Often followed by "to" when comparing to a standard
> 3. Separable when transitive: "measure the candidate up" or "measure up the candidate"
> 4. Pattern extends to: measure up against, measure up to

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** To measure the physical dimensions of something
> - **Figurative:** To meet expectations, standards, or requirements; to be good enough
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To evaluate performance or quality against a benchmark
> - **Nuance:** Implies comparison to an external standard or expectation
>
