# crusty debris

> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Adjective + Noun Compound Pattern
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Adjective] + [Noun]
> crusty + debris
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** [Adjective] (crusty) - describing texture/consistency
> - **Component 2:** [Noun] (debris) - the material/substance being described
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Simple adjective-noun combination
> 2. "Crusty" modifies "debris" to describe hardened, dried residue
> 3. No hyphen needed as it's straightforward modification
> 4. Can be made plural: "crusty debris" (mass noun, rarely pluralized)

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** Hardened, dry pieces of waste or scattered remains
> - **Figurative:** Stubborn remnants or lingering traces of something unpleasant
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To describe dried, hardened residue or unwanted material
> - **Nuance:** Slightly negative connotation, implies need for cleaning
