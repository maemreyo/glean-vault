to chart a path forward
> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Idiomatic Expression / Infinitive Phrase
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> to + chart + a + path + forward
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **"to chart"**: Infinitive verb meaning to map or plan
> - **"a path"**: Noun phrase representing a course or way
> - **"forward"**: Adverb indicating future progress
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Usually follows modal verbs (need to, must to, have to)
> 2. Can be used with various subjects (we, they, the company)
> 3. Fixed word order - cannot be rearranged

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** To create a map showing the way ahead
> - **Figurative:** To plan a course of action for future progress
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To emphasize strategic planning and future direction
> - **Nuance:** Professional, formal, often used in business/leadership contexts
>
