> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Phrasal Verb (Verb + Particle/Adverb)
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Subject] + put + down + [Object]
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Verb (put):** Action of placing or setting
> - **Particle (down):** Directional modifier indicating lower position
> - **Object:** What is being put down (optional with some meanings)
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Transitive: requires object when meaning "place on surface"
> 2. Separable: object can go between verb and particle
> 3. Multiple meanings with different grammatical patterns

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal 1:** To place something on a surface
> - **Literal 2:** To write something on paper
> - **Figurative 1:** To criticize or insult someone
> - **Figurative 2:** To euthanize an animal
> - **Figurative 3:** To pay money as a deposit
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** Various actions depending on context
> - **Nuance:** Context determines meaning - can be neutral or negative
>

**Answer::** [Subject] + put + down + [Object]

### Card 2: Meaning
**Question::** What does **put down** express?
**Answer::** Multiple meanings: place something, write something, criticize someone, or euthanize an animal

### Card 3: Usage
**Question::** When should you use **put down**?
**Answer::** Context determines meaning - for placing objects, writing information, criticizing, or medical euthanasia

### Card 4: Example
**Question::** Create a sentence with **put down** about writing
**Answer::** The teacher asked us to put down our answers on the answer sheet.

### Card 5: Error Correction
**Question::** What's wrong with: "He put down his friend"
**Answer::** Ambiguous. Could mean wrote about friend OR criticized friend. Clarify: "He wrote down his friend's number" or "He put his friend down with harsh words."

### Card 6: Comparison
**Question::** How is **put down** different from [[write down]]?
**Answer::** "Write down" specifically means to record in writing, while "put down" has multiple meanings including placement and criticism

**Answer::** Put down your name here.
