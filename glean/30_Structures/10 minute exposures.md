10 minute exposures
> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Compound Noun Phrase / Technical Term
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Number: 10] + [Time Unit: minute] + [Plural Noun: exposures]
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** `10 minute` - hyphenated compound adjective (when before noun)
> - **Component 2:** `exposures` - plural noun indicating instances of being exposed
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Hyphenate when used as adjective before noun: "10-minute exposure"
> 2. No hyphen when used as noun phrase: "exposures of 10 minutes"
> 3. Plural "exposures" indicates multiple instances

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** Multiple instances of being subjected to something for 10 minutes each
> - **Figurative:** Brief but repeated encounters with situations or conditions
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To quantify duration and frequency of exposure
> - **Nuance:** Technical, precise, clinical or scientific
>
