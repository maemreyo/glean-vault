> **Structure Type:** Noun Phrase / Descriptive Pattern

> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> a + [sensory adjective] + [natural formation noun]
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** Indefinite article "a" - introduces one instance
> - **Component 2:** Sensory adjective "glittering" - describes visual effect of light reflection
> - **Component 3:** Natural formation noun "cavern" - large cave or underground chamber
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Use "a" for singular, countable nouns when first mentioning
> 2. Sensory adjectives (glittering, sparkling, shining) precede the noun
> 3. "Glittering" is present participle used as adjective
> 4. Pattern extends to: a + [visual adjective] + [natural place]
