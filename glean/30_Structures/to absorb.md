> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Infinitive Verb Pattern
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> to + [verb]
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** Infinitive marker "to" - indicates base form of verb
> - **Component 2:** Base verb "absorb" - action of taking in or assimilating
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Use "to" before base form of verb to create infinitive
> 2. No "-s", "-ing", or "-ed" endings in infinitive form
> 3. Pattern functions as noun, adjective, or adverb in sentences

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** To take in or soak up (liquid, light, knowledge)
> - **Figurative:** To fully engage with, understand, or assimilate information/experiences
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To express the action or process of absorption
> - **Nuance:** Can describe physical, mental, or emotional taking in
