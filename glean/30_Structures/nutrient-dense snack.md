# nutrient-dense snack

> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Compound Adjective + Noun Pattern
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Adjective] + [-] + [Noun] + [Noun]
> nutrient + - + dense + snack
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** [Adjective] + [-] + [Noun] (nutrient-dense) - compound adjective describing quality
> - **Component 2:** [Noun] (snack) - the main item being described
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. First noun acts as adjective modifier to second noun
> 2. Hyphen joins the noun-adjective pair when they modify the main noun together
> 3. No hyphen when the first noun modifies the second noun separately (nutrient dense snack = snack that is dense in nutrients)

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** A small food item that contains a high concentration of nutrients
> - **Figurative:** Something small but highly valuable or beneficial
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To describe food that provides maximum nutritional value in small portions
> - **Nuance:** Informal to semi-formal, emphasizes health consciousness and efficiency
>
