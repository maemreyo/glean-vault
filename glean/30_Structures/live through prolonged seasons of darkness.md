> [!info] Structural Analysis

> ```text

>
> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** Phrasal Verb "live through" - to survive or endure difficult experiences
> - **Component 2:** Adjective "prolonged" - describing extended duration
> - **Component 3:** Noun "seasons" - recurring time periods
> - **Component 4:** Preposition "of" - showing relationship/contents
> - **Component 5:** Noun "darkness" - absence of light, metaphor for hardship
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Phrasal verb is intransitive here (no direct object)
> 2. Adjectives precede the nouns they modify
> 3. "Of" connects contents to container (darkness to seasons)
> 4. Collective meaning created by phrase combination

> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** To survive extended periods without sunlight
> - **Figurative:** To endure long-lasting difficult times or depression
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To express resilience through extended hardship
> - **Nuance:** Literary or formal register, evokes emotional endurance
