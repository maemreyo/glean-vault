# rich in plant-based protein

> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Adjective + Preposition + Compound Noun Pattern
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Adjective] + [Preposition] + [Adjective] + [-] + [Noun]
> rich + in + plant + - + based + protein
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** [Adjective] + [Preposition] (rich in) - expressing abundance
> - **Component 2:** [Compound Adjective] (plant-based) - specifying source/type
> - **Component 3:** [Noun] (protein) - the substance being described
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. "Rich in" is a fixed adjective-preposition combination
> 2. "Plant-based" is hyphenated when used as adjective before noun
> 3. The structure emphasizes the source and abundance simultaneously
> 4. Can be modified: "rich in plant-based proteins" (plural)

> - **Literal:** Containing high amounts of protein derived from plant sources
> - **Figurative:** Abundantly supplied with vegetarian protein sources
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To highlight nutritional value from non-animal sources
> - **Nuance:** Health-conscious, vegan/vegetarian friendly, sustainable
