being cooked alive
> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Idiom / Metaphorical Expression
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Being] + [Past Participle: cooked] + [Adverb: alive]
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** `being cooked` - passive continuous action of cooking
> - **Component 2:** `alive` - state of living while experiencing the action
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Fixed expression with specific word order
> 2. Used metaphorically for intense, prolonged suffering
> 3. Cannot be modified (no adjectives before 'cooked')

> [!abstract] Meaning & Usage
> **Core Meaning:**
> - **Literal:** The process of cooking something while it's still living
> - **Figurative:** Experiencing extreme, slow, torturous discomfort or suffering
>
> **Functional Meaning:**
> - **Purpose:** To exaggerate discomfort, emphasize intense suffering
> - **Nuance:** Hyperbolic, dramatic, intense
>
