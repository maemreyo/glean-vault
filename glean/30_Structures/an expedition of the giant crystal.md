> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Complex Noun Phrase / Adventure Narrative Pattern

> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Indefinite Article: an] + [Activity: expedition] + [Preposition: of] + [Descriptive Phrase: the giant crystal]
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Component 1:** `an expedition` - organized journey or exploration for a purpose
> - **Component 2:** `of the giant crystal` - specifies the objective or destination
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Use "an" before expedition (vowel sound)
> 2. "Expedition of" indicates purpose or objective
> 3. Definite article "the" with specific crystal

> - **Figurative:** A quest or search for something magnificent and valuable
>
> **Functional Meaning:**

> - **Nuance:** Adventurous, romantic, quest-like
