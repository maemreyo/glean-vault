> [!info] Structural Analysis
> **Structure Type:** Noun Phrase (Article + Adjective + Noun)
>
> **Formula / Pattern:**
> ```text
> [Indefinite Article: a/an] + [Adjective: crab] + [Noun: spider]
> ```
>
> **Components:**
> - **Article (a):** Indefinite article indicating one of many
> - **Adjective (crab):** Descriptive modifier comparing to crab characteristics
> - **Noun (spider):** Core noun referring to arachnid species
>
> **Formation Rules:**
> 1. Use "a" before consonant sounds
> 2. Compound adjective "crab" functions as a species descriptor
> 3. No hyphen required as "crab" functions as a standard adjective
